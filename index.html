Explore Norway

Troms og Finnmark:
Gateway to the Arctic

Located in Norway's far north, Troms og Finnmark is a land of unparalleled Arctic beauty. This region offers breathtaking landscapes that range from dramatic fjords and rugged mountains to the vast tundra of Finnmark. As Norway’s largest and northernmost county, it is renowned for its vibrant Sami culture, thrilling outdoor adventures, and awe-inspiring natural phenomena like the Northern Lights in winter and the Midnight Sun in summer.

What makes it unique

Northern Lights Capital

Tromsø, often called the "Gateway to the Arctic," is one of the best places on Earth to witness the Aurora Borealis.

Midnight Sun

In the summer, the sun never sets, providing endless daylight for exploration.

Sami Heritage

The region is deeply rooted in Sami traditions, offering opportunities to meet reindeer herders, learn about indigenous culture, and experience traditional joik singing.

Wildlife Haven

From whale watching to spotting Arctic foxes, Troms og Finnmark is a haven for nature lovers.
